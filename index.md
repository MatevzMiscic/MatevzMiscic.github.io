### Research
I am a PhD student at University of Ljubljana. My current research interests lie in **Group Theory** and **Combinatorics**, with a particular focus on the **Babai's Conjecture**. My advisor is Urban Jezernik.

#### Publications & Preprints

* **Diameter bounds for finite simple Lie algebras** (with Marco Barbieri and Urban Jezernik).  
  *Preprint* (2025). [[arXiv]](https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.15351)

* **Random Lie bracket on $\mathfrak{sl}_2(\mathbb{F}_p)$** (with Urban Jezernik).  
  *Random Structures & Algorithms* (2025). [[Journal]](https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1002/rsa.70042) [[arXiv]](https://arxiv.org/abs/2503.16175)
